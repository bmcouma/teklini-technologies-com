Meet the Founder

Transforming businesses through innovative technology solutions and digital empowerment.

Bravin M. Ouma is a passionate technology entrepreneur and full-stack developer with extensive experience in creating digital solutions that drive business success. With a background spanning web development, IT infrastructure, artificial intelligence, and digital marketing, Bravin founded Teklini Technologies to bridge the digital divide and empower organizations across Kenya and beyond.

His expertise encompasses modern web technologies, cloud infrastructure, AI integration, and business automation. Bravin is committed to delivering solutions that are not only technically excellent but also accessible and tailored to each client's unique needs.
